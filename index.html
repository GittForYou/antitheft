How Thieves Steal Your Car Without Touching It

They don't break windows or hotwire ignition. They use sophisticated relay devices to amplify your key fob's signal from inside your home.

The Relay Attack Explained

  • Signal Capture: Device captures your key fob signal from inside your house
  • Signal Relay: Transmits that signal to your car in your driveway
  • Unlock: Car thinks key is present and unlocks
  • Start: Pressing brake/start button begins the theft
  • Drive Away: Car is gone in under 10 seconds with no evidence
